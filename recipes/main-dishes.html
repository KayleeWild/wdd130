Tips For Frying Stuff

The first recipe is all about how to fry stuff. Periodically at Apt. 6, we would observe a holiday that we referred to as “fry stuff Friday.” It happened on Fridays (or other days of the week if we couldn’t wait) and in honor of the name we would fry stuff. Pretty straightforward. To help others observe the time-honored tradition of “fry stuff Friday”, we included the basic guidelines for frying food and a list of suggested ingredients. Just make sure you don't fry potatoes when Lyle is home... unless you like being a human punch-bag.

How To Fry Stuff

Fried Stuff

Ingredients:

Oil
Pancake Mix
Water
Eggs
Flour
seasonings
literally anything you want to fry

Instructions:

1. Heat oil 350 degrees
2. Mix pancake mix and water into bowl until smooth
3. For non-sweet batter make an egg wash by beating eggs in a bowl.
4. In another bowl mix flour and seasonings
5. Dip items in mixture then place into oil
6. Cook each side for 20-30 seconds or until Golden Brown

"Fried and True" Ideas:

fish
cookie dough
starbursts (inside cookie dough)
pickles
onion rings
uncrustables (best without batter or in air-fryer)
OREOs

J Pops / J Pop Burgers

J Pops (Jalapeño Poppers)

Ingredients:

10 Jalapeños
2 Cream Cheese packages (8oz)
20 slices of Bacon
Spice Blend
Green Onion

Instructions:

1. Cut Jalapenos in half and clean out insides and wash
2. Soften Cream cheese add spice mix and chopped green onions or chives then mix in bowl
3. Then stuff Jalapenos with cream cheese filling
4. Wrap in Bacon and secure with toothpick
5. Smoke on low 20 minutes on both sides
6. Then broil on low until bacon is crispy

J Pop Burger

Ingredients:

½ pound of ground beef
Cream cheese
Jalapenos
Spice blend
Pepper Jack cheese
Bun
4 Slices of Bacon

Instructions:

1. Spilt ½ pound of ground beef into balls and form into flat patties
2. Dice Jalapenos then mix with cream cheese and shredded pepper jack cheese in bowl
3. Place a dollop of mixture onto one of the patties then cover with the other patties and seal the edges until it looks like a thick normal pattie.
4. Wrap the patties then in a woven blanket of bacon then secure with toothpicks
5. Smoke on low for 30 minutes on each side then broil on low until bacon is crispy
6. Then add more cheese on top if wanted and then add to a toasted bun

Pulled-Pork Mac 'n Cheese

Pulled Pork Mac 'n Cheese

Ingredients:

1 Box of Shell pasta
Milk
2 tablespoons of butter
Cheese
Spices (garlic powder, oregano, and paprika)
Pork Loin
Worcestershire Sauce
Liquid Smoke

Instructions:

Mac and Cheese:
Start with a pot and put your macaroni in there, then fill it with milk until it covers the noodles. Put it on a medium high heat and stir it constantly.
Once the noodles are cooked, turn the heat to low.
Add 2 tablespoons of butter and as much cheese as your heart desires and stir it together until it's smooth.
Then add some spices, add garlic powder, oregano, paprika, and others.

Pulled Pork:
Set Pork Loin into an Instant Pot on low for 8 hours with spices, liquid smoke, Worcestershire sauce.
Then shred the pork


Add ½ of Mac and cheese into a baking dish then add the pulled pork on top then the rest of the Mac and Cheese and sprinkle more cheese on top and bake at 350° until the cheese is melted.

Tater-Tot Casserole

Tater-Tot Casserole

Ingredients:

3 Cups cooked Chicken (shredded or chopped)
10.5oz can of Cream of Chicken soup
½ cup of Grated Cheddar Cheese
½ cup of Grated Pepper Jack Cheese
16 oz of Sour Cream
1 package of Ranch dressing Mix
Spices (Dan-o’s and Garlic and Herb)
1 cup of Chopped Bacon
1 pound of frozen Tater Tots
1 Family Size bag of Doritos

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and spray an 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray
2. Cook chicken and shred and air fry tater tots.
3. In a mixing bowl mix together cream of chicken, ½ the cheese, sour cream, chopped Bacon, and spices.
4. In a pan layer tots the doritos then chicken then doritos then tots. Then bake for 35 minutes
5. Then sprinkle more cheese on top and cook again for 5-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

Tofu Stuffed Peppers*

Tofu Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:

8 Jalapenos
1 Block of Tofu
Liquid Smoke
Worcestershire sauce
Spices (Dan-o’s, Devil’s spit, Garlic and Herb)
Shredded Cheese
2 oz Cream Cheese

Instructions:

1. Cut Jalapenos in half and clean out insides and wash
2. Smoke the tofu for 30 minutes on each side
3. Then cook tofu in skillet with spices, Worcestershire sauce, Cream Cheese, and more in needed
4. Then stuff Jalapenos and set oven to broil on low
5. Bake until peppers are cooked then top with some shredded cheese.

Smoked Chicken Alfredo

Smoked Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

3-4 pounds of Chicken thighs
1 Box of Pasta
2 cups of Milk
3 oz of Cream Cheese
3 Tablespoons of Flour
1 Teaspoon of Salt
1 Tablespoon of Butter
3 cloves of garlic
1 cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
Spice Blend
1 Can of Mt. Dew
BBQ Sauce

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken in Mt. Dew, BBQ Sauce and Spices overnight to 2 days.
2. Smoke on Low for 30 minutes each side until cooked
3. Place milk, cream cheese, flour, and salt in a blender and blend until smooth.
4. Boil pasta until noodles are cooked.
5. In a saucepan melt butter and add garlic on medium heat
6. Then add milk mixture into saucepan and stir constantly for 3-4 minutes or until thick and remove from heat and add cheese
7. Chop up the chicken then add to noodles along with the saucepan mixture.

Shrimp Crawfish Boil

Shrimp Crawfish Boil

Ingredients:

2 pounds of Shrimp
3 pounds of Crawfish
4 Lemons
12 cloves of Garlic
2-3 gallons of Water
2 pounds of Red Potatoes
1 Yellow Onion
2 pounds of Sausage
6 Ears of Corn
Spices (Cajun seasoning,Garlic Powder, LemonPepper, Worcestershire Sauce, Blackpepper )
1 Stick of Butter

Instructions:

1. Boil the Water with lemons, onions, garlic, potatoes, and spices
2. When potatoes are almost cooked and at a roiling boil add crawfish, sausage, and corn
3. Wait until roiling boil again wait 15 minutes then add shrimp
4. Once shrimp is cooked, drain then pot and then throw on table and eat.

Sushi Bowls*

Sushi Bowls

Ingredients:

1/4 cup of dry Rice
1/2 cup of Water
1 package of Imitation Crab (3 oz)
Coconut Amino
1/2 Avocado
1/2 Cucumber
1/2 Lemon
Dried Seaweed
2 Tbsp Yum Yum Sauce

Instructions:

1. Cook rice. Put rice and water in an uncovered pot and boil on high. Once boiling, turn to low and let sit, covered, for 20 minutes. Don't stir. Fluff with fork before serving.
2. While rice is cooking, dice the cucumber, avocado, and crab. Cut lemon into extremely thin wedges.
3. Assemble sushi bowl by placing rice in the bottom. Then add coconut aminos until brown (don't overdo it!). Crumble seaweed and sprinkle on top. Then put crab on top, followed by a drizzle of Yum Yum sauce. Finish with the lemons, cucumbers and avocados on top.

* Denotes a healthier "402 Recipe"
